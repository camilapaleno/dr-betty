Nutrition

Focusing on: Replacing Old Habits with Healthy New Ones!



  • Metabolic Disorders
  • Weight Loss or Weight Gain
  • Menopause
  • Loss of Energy
  • Stress
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Post-Pathology

  • Thyroid Dysfunction
  • Pregnancy

Fulfill your health potential! Essential Health offers a unique program for the achievement of a healthier, more vibrant life.

Each patient receives individual counseling.

Dr. Ehrlich designs personalized nutrition programs to restore your health on a cellular level. We offer an array of all-natural supplements that aid in the correction of underlying health problems - when applicable.

Essential Health uses a computer-assisted body composition analysis and exclusive symptom-prioritizing software.

We listen to what your goals and needs are. Everyone is individually assessed to work at your own pace and lifestyle.

This process is divided into two parts. An initial visit to gather all information and a follow-up visit to deliver your unique plan.



Nutrition Consultation - What to expect on your first nutritional visit:



A healthy combination of motivation, the right foods, specific supplements when needed, exercise, and replacing some unhealthy habits with healthy new ones!

Typically, your first nutritional visit will review the following:



  • Complete History
  • Goals
  • Symptom Survey
  • Exam - Physical
  • Muscle Testing
  • Review of Recent Lab work
  • Review of Medical Records
  • Vital signs
  • Body Measurements
    • BMI - Body Mass Index
    • Fat Percentage
    • Ph Levels
    • Vital Lung Capacity


Nutrition Report - What to expect on your second nutritional visit:



Good nutrition does not come with fad diets or miracle pills, but rather by focusing on quality rather than quantity of real food!

What should I buy? Where? How much? Is there really a difference between organic and genetically modified produce?

All these questions and more can be answered by Dr. Ehrlich!

Health is a combination of the well being of the mind and body.

On your second visit Dr. Ehrlich will present you with "your book" - your personalized nutrition plan where she has written down everything she advises.

You will typically discuss:



  • Current Nutrition and Fitness Goals
  • Exercise
  • Focused Food List
  • Food - Do's & Don'ts
  • Lifestyle Suggestions
  • Meal Plans
  • Menu - What to select at restaurants
  • Pertinent Research or Related Articles
  • Recipes - Try cooking some of Dr. Ehrlich's favorites!
  • Various approaches to what may seem most challenging
